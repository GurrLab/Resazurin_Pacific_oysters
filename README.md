# Resazurin_Pacific_oysters

### Objective: 

Screen  metabolic performance of Pacific oyster *Magallana* (*Crassostrea*) *gigas* larvae and seed produced by the year cohort 2026-2027 from USDA ARS Pacific Shellfish Research Unit. 

### Aims: 

(1) Develop and refine methodologies to employ resazurin assays in larvae and alongside a breeding center timeline

(2) Determine if we detect a signal from larvae and seed, amidst the constraints of a a functioning hatchery.

(3) Determine whether resazurin is effective in detecting differences between selectively-bred oyster families.
